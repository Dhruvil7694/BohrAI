The paper architect agent sits between planning and drafting.

## What it does

It turns a topic plus evidence inventory into a manuscript blueprint:

- working title
- paper type
- section map
- claim-to-evidence map
- figure and table opportunities
- reviewer-risk notes

## Why it matters

This agent keeps the writer from outrunning the evidence. If the source base is narrow, the architecture should narrow the manuscript too.

## Output

A structured paper blueprint, usually written to `outputs/.plans/<slug>-architecture.md`.

## Used by

Most directly used in `/paper-pro`, and suitable anywhere Bohr needs to turn evidence into a disciplined manuscript structure before prose drafting starts.
