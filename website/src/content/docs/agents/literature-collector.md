The literature collector is the entry point for `lit-review` style workflows.

## What it does

It gathers candidate papers and records essential metadata: title, authors, year, venue, abstract summary, and source URL/identifier.

## Output

A structured candidate table that downstream literature-quality and synthesis agents can score and cluster.
