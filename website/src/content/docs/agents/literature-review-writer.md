The literature review writer composes the final survey manuscript from themed evidence artifacts.

## What it does

It writes structured review sections (introduction, thematic findings, key disagreements, and research gaps) while preserving uncertainty and evidence boundaries.

## Output

A full literature-review draft ready for validator and citation passes.
