The literature synthesizer converts a filtered paper list into a coherent theme map.

## What it does

It clusters papers by research direction, method family, or evaluation style, then summarizes consensus and divergence within each theme.

## Output

A theme-to-paper mapping and thematic summaries for the final review writer.
